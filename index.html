How It Works

Installing Your Unique Ice Rink

1

Initial Phone Call

Pick up the phone and give us a call — even if you're just thinking about a project.

2

Building Your Estimate

Once we have a general idea of your project, we'll provide a rough, itemized estimate.

3

First Site Visit

We'll schedule a site visit to take measurements and discuss unique features you'd like.

4

Crafting the Proposal

HINA will provide a detailed proposal encompassing all aspects of the project.

5

Gather Equipment

Upon receipt of a signed proposal and deposit, we order your custom ice rink. 12–15 weeks lead time.

6

Site Prep & Installation

We work to have you up and skating as soon as the weather turns cold.
